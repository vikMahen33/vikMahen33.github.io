StoryChatter.Com

I Pioneered Its Creation And Managed The Creation Of The Animated Videos and Voice-Overs. Co-Founder

Ever felt as if your child is unable to connect with his or her culture? With Storychatter, your child can form meaningful and long lasting connections with his or her heritage. This allows for your child to mirror the same pride that you have in the nation, religion, or leaders that made you, you. Spreading this sense of pride and recognition is not only limited to your child; a portion of our profits from your subscriptions will go towards one less fortunate child in India gaining the education that everyone rightfully deserves. At StoryChatter we have made it a goal to bring this ideal forth in those children in India who do not have the ability or opportunity to grow or flourish due to their lack of education. All children should have the opportunity to connect with their heritage, and thrive under the right teachings, and Story Chatter’s main objective is to make this a reality.

City Speaks

Complete IOS Front-End And Back-End Development

Ever wanted to discover the town you live in? Find new people, clubs, and organizations in your area? Instantly discover and chat with people in your area? City Speaks uses technology to enable a close-knit, and wise communnity, which modern day life is lacking, while ensuring safety through location based emergency warnings. It features a simple, intuitive user interface in which there are chatrooms both public and private (based on the preference of its creator) which are tagged with locations and only appear to those within the specified region, to ensure only people of the same community can view the chatroom. City Speaks also allows you to discover people around you and chat with them.

Do your friends never return your favors? Ever find yourself doing more favors than you receive? Or have trouble keeping track of who owes you what? UOI aims to eliminate just that! UOI also provides paticence and credibility ratings for your friends, so you know exactly who you are dealing with. By implementing a modern and simple UI, it enables you to keep of everything.
